Hello

I’m Paige, a Freelance Digital Designer and Interaction Design Graduate.

My passion is designing. Whether it's for the web or print, I am constantly experimenting and trying out new techniques and ideas to better my skills as a creative.

I graduated with a First Class Honours degree in BDes (Hons) Interaction Design in July 2018. During my degree, I developed an interest in user interface design. I love pushing the boundaries of what a website or app is expected to look like by playing around with dynamic and modern designs or incorporating illustrations.

I have also completed an 11 month placement with Ulster University as their Web & Multimedia Content Designer. At the moment I'm working as a freelance digital designer, and on the lookout for a full-time graduate position in UX Design, UI Design or Product Design. Feel free to get in touch with any opportunities!

In my final year of university I focused on designing an iOS app and conceptual Augmented Reality product which explores how immersive tech could improve communication between Deaf and Hearing children. If you're keen to see more, I blogged about my process on Medium!
