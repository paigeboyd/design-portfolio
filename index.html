Hello

I’m Paige, an Interaction Design Student and aspiring UI Designer.

My passion is designing. Whether it's for the web or print, I am constantly experimenting and trying out new techniques and ideas to better my skills as a designer.

I'm currently a final year student on BDes (Hons) Interaction Design at Belfast School of Art. Since beginning my degree, I've developed a passion for user interface design. I love pushing the boundaries of what a website is expected to look like by playing around with dynamic and modern designs or incorporating illustrations. This past year I completed an 11 month placement with Ulster University as their Web & Multimedia Content Designer. At the moment I'm working as a freelance digital designer!

When I’m not focusing on university or designing, I’m most likely doodling in a sketchbook, taking photos of the sunset or on Instagram!
