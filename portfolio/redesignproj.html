Redesign Project

The Redesign Project is a side project I started to allow myself the opportunity to explore different UI Designs and to improve my skills. The project is simply me picking a brand and redesigning their website or app. With no restrictions or briefs holding me back, it means I can see where my creativity takes the design - sometimes it's the homepage, other times just one element to the site.

Clicking on the images will bring you to Dribbble to see them in more detail.

Why a redesign?

I wanted to have a side project that allowed me to explore and practice what I'm interested in. User Interface Design has been where my interest has steered towards from I began my university degree. I wanted a side project that didn't constrain me to deadlines or to necessarily have a 'finished product'. I see this project as ongoing; something I can continue to add to for as long as I want. Redesigning brands that are already out there and often that are already well established presents a unique challenge in itself - how can I improve this? What hasn't already been done?


The Process

I don't have a strict process for this project either. Sometimes ideas come to me when I'm doodling in my sketchbook, other times I come up with them when I'm experimenting with layouts in Photoshop/Illustrator. I tend to create the final designs within Adobe Illustrator as it allows me the most control over the design, and means that I can create vector illustrations within the design if I need to.
